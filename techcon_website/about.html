History

The name "Techcon" is associated with several different companies, each with its own history. Techcon Systems, founded in 1961, focuses on fluid dispensing solutions for industrial manufacturing. Techcon Global, established in 2019, organizes events connecting entrepreneurs and investors. Techcon-nv, a consultancy, has evolved since 1996 from a technical office to a broader consultancy. Additionally, TechCon North America has been held since 1996, focusing on utility topics and technology.

SRC has a rich history of success. Since 1982, SRC has funded more than $2.5B in research, built a semiconductor workforce by sponsoring more than 16,000 graduate students, and provided over 890 patents to member companies.

For almost 40 years, SRC and its members have assembled the best university researchers while educating an elite workforce of talented graduate students—in science, engineering, and technology. SRC graduates accrue a competitive advantage over their non-SRC counterparts, and SRC member organizations secure early access to an expanding set of solutions addressing their deepest technical challenges.

Mission

SRC is a world-renowned, high technology-based consortium that serves as a crossroads of collaboration between technology companies, academia, government agencies, and SRC is highly regarded engineers and scientists. Through its interdisciplinary research programs, SRC plays an indispensable role in addressing global challenges, using research and development strategies, and advanced tools and technologies. Members of SRC work synergistically together, gain access to research results, fundamental IP, and highly experienced students to compete in the global marketplace and build the workforce of tomorrow.

Past Speakers

TechCon 2024 featured two exceptional speakers who delivered insightful presentations:

Nido Qubein

Dr. Nido Qubein came to the United States as a teenager with little knowledge of English and only $50 in his pocket. His journey has been an amazing success story. The Biography Channel aired his life story titled “A Life of Success and Significance.”

As an educator, he is president of High Point University, an undergraduate and graduate institution with 4,300 students from 40 countries. He has authored two dozen books and audio programs distributed worldwide.

As a business leader, he is chairman of Great Harvest Bread Company with 220 stores in 43 states. He serves on the boards of several national organizations including BB&T (a Fortune 500 company with $185 billion in assets), the La-Z-Boy Corporation (one of the largest and most recognized furniture brands worldwide) and Dots Stores (a chain of fashion boutiques with more than 400 locations across the country).

Ajit Manocha

Mr. Manocha is a veteran semiconductor industry executive with more than 30 years of global expertise in Manufacturing Operations, General Management, and Semiconductor Technology. He has a wealth of talent, experience and leadership ability.

Mr. Manocha has very recently retired after serving as the Chief Executive Officer of Global Foundries since 2011 and now serving as Strategic Advisor to GLOBALFOUNDRIES. Prior to his tenure as GLOBALFOUNDRIES's CEO, Mr. Manocha was Senior Advisor to GLOBALFOUNDRIES's investors ATIC/Mubadala for a couple of years.

Mr. Manocha served as Executive Vice President of Worldwide Operations at Spansion Inc. from December 2007 to February, 2010. Mr. Manocha was responsible for all aspects of Spansion's global manufacturing operations, IT and supply chain management, including product cost, yields, quality, reliability, on-time delivery, manufacturing cycle times and customer service.
